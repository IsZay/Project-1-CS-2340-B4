About GTMovies

As Georgia Tech students, we understand the importance of balancing work and relaxation. With our busy schedules and academic stress, finding time to unwind is essential. GTMovies provides a space where students can view, rent, and review movies, creating a shared entertainment experience within the Georgia Tech community.
